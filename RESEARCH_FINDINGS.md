# AfricanKingsEshop - Research Findings

## African Men's Fashion Styles

### Core Garment Types

#### 1. **Agbada**
- Traditional West African robe, particularly from Nigeria and Ghana
- Features: Long flowing outer garment, often paired with embroidered shirts and pants
- Embellishments: Gold embroidery, intricate patterns, beadwork
- Occasions: Weddings, formal events, cultural ceremonies
- Color palette: Black with gold, white, navy with gold accents, burgundy
- Modern fusion: Tailored fits, contemporary embroidery patterns

#### 2. **Dashiki**
- Loose-fitting shirt with geometric or tribal patterns
- Features: V-neck or round neck, often with embroidery on chest and sleeves
- Versatility: Can be worn casually or formally depending on fabric quality
- Patterns: Geometric designs, tribal motifs, Kente patterns
- Modern versions: Fitted cuts, mixed with contemporary fabrics

#### 3. **Buba**
- Traditional Yoruba shirt, typically worn with Sokoto (matching pants)
- Features: Loose-fitting, embroidered chest panel, decorative sleeves
- Embroidery: Often features intricate hand-stitched patterns
- Occasions: Formal events, weddings, cultural celebrations
- Modern adaptations: Contemporary embroidery, premium fabrics

#### 4. **Kaftan/Boubou**
- Long, flowing robe-like garment
- Features: Loose silhouette, often with embroidery
- Versatility: Can be dressed up or down
- Embellishments: Gold embroidery, beading, appliqué
- Occasions: Formal events, cultural celebrations, modern casual wear

#### 5. **Senator Wear**
- Modern tailored suit inspired by traditional African styles
- Features: Matching shirt and pants with embroidered neckline
- Occasions: Formal events, weddings, professional settings
- Embroidery: Prominent on chest and cuffs
- Colors: Navy, black, gold, burgundy with contrasting embroidery

### Design Elements & Aesthetics

#### Color Palette
- **Bold & Vibrant**: Gold, burgundy, navy, emerald green, bright blue
- **Neutral Bases**: Black, white, cream with bold accents
- **Ankara Prints**: Geometric patterns, tribal motifs, floral designs
- **Metallic Accents**: Gold embroidery, beading, sequins

#### Embroidery & Patterns
- **Geometric Patterns**: Kente patterns, tribal designs, symmetrical motifs
- **Embroidery Techniques**: Hand-stitched, machine embroidery, beadwork
- **Appliqué**: Layered fabrics creating depth and texture
- **Metallic Threads**: Gold and silver embroidery for formal wear

#### Fabrics
- **Premium Cotton**: Breathable, comfortable for formal events
- **Ankara/Wax Print**: Bold geometric patterns on cotton
- **Damask**: Textured fabric with raised patterns
- **Silk Blends**: For luxury formal wear
- **Brocade**: Rich, textured fabric with metallic threads

### Modern Fusion Trends
- Combining traditional embroidery with contemporary tailoring
- Mixing Ankara prints with modern silhouettes
- Blending traditional motifs with minimalist designs
- Contemporary color combinations with traditional patterns
- Hybrid styles: Dashiki-inspired shirts with modern pants

## Product Categories for E-Commerce

### 1. **Traditional Formal Wear** (Agbada, Buba)
- Complete 3-piece sets (shirt, pants, outer robe)
- Premium embroidery
- Occasion-specific (weddings, ceremonies)
- Price range: $150-$400 USD / ₦60,000-₦160,000 NGN

### 2. **Modern Fusion Shirts** (Dashiki, Kaftan)
- Contemporary cuts with traditional patterns
- Versatile for casual and semi-formal
- Mix of traditional and modern fabrics
- Price range: $50-$150 USD / ₦20,000-₦60,000 NGN

### 3. **Senator Wear Sets**
- Tailored shirt and pants combinations
- Professional and formal occasions
- Premium embroidery details
- Price range: $120-$300 USD / ₦48,000-₦120,000 NGN

### 4. **Accessories**
- Embroidered caps/hats
- Matching shoes
- Beaded accessories
- Belts and fasteners
- Price range: $20-$80 USD / ₦8,000-₦32,000 NGN
